ABOUT US

Team Therosel is a TU/e based team focused on the topic of thermochemical energy storage. We provide a free-to-use online dashboard that helps homeowners explore what effect thermochemical energy storage can have on their home. We were started in 2021 as an Honors team with four students. In addition to this dashboard, we collect the latest innovation in the field of thermochemical energy storage.
